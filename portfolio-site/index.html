About

A designer built on listening.

I spent three years as a Design Consultant at Ethan Allen where I had the privilege of working with over 200 clients on everything from single-room refreshes to full-home transformations. The common thread in every project was the same: the design only works when it fits how the client actually lives.

Before Ethan Allen, I supported a principal designer at Goff Designs on boutique residential projects and coordinated client operations at Vintage Design Inc. That combination of firsthand design execution, detailed project coordination, and high-touch client engagement is what I bring to every consultation.

I hold a Bachelor of Business Administration from Arizona State University and I'm based in Rancho Santa Margarita, serving clients throughout Orange County and beyond.
