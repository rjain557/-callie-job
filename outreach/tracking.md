## Status values
- **Sent** — outreach sent
- **Reply +** — positive response
- **Reply -** — not interested, no fit
- **Scheduled** — call/meeting on calendar
- **Met** — conversation happened
- **Intro made** — they referred you to someone
- **Closed** — ghosted after 10 days

## Weekly review questions
At end of each week, ask:
1. Which template got the highest response rate?
2. Which outreach type led to most intros/interviews?
3. What patterns do we see in "Reply +" responses?
4. Where should we focus effort next week?
