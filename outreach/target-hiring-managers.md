# Target Hiring Manager List — Orange County Design Companies

How to use this list:
1. Before outreach, verify each contact is still at the company (LinkedIn)
2. Personalize Template 1 (`email-templates/01-hiring-manager-direct.md`)
3. Send on Tues-Thurs between 9-11am (best open rates)
4. Log every send in `tracking.md`
5. Follow up after 5 business days if no response

**Email format guessing rules** (most companies):
- `firstname.lastname@company.com`
- `firstname@company.com`
- `firstinitial+lastname@company.com`
- `info@company.com` as fallback (lower response rate)

Use hunter.io or voilanorbert.com free tier to verify email format before sending.

## Outreach campaign plan (30 days)

**Week 1 — Preparation**
- Complete LinkedIn audit (see `linkedin-audit.md`)
- Deploy portfolio site (see `portfolio-site/`)
- Send first 3 past-client referral emails (Template 2)

**Week 2 — Direct Hiring Manager Outreach**
- Send 5 hiring manager emails to Tier 1 companies (Template 1)
- Wait for responses, log in `tracking.md`

**Week 3 — Recruiter Engagement**
- Reach out to 3 recruiters (see `recruiters.md`)
- Send 3 alumni reconnect emails (Template 3)

**Week 4 — Follow-up and Trade Visit**
- Follow up on any unanswered outreach (Template 1 follow-up)
- Visit Laguna Design Center in person (Tier 3 showrooms)
- Add 5 more companies to outreach

**Expected outcomes (based on industry norms):**
- 15-20% response rate to direct outreach = 2-4 conversations from Week 2
- 30-50% response rate to past-client referral = 2-5 intros
- 40-60% response rate to alumni reconnect = 2-4 conversations
- 1-2 recruiter relationships active by end of Week 2
- **Target: 3-5 screening calls per week by Week 4**

## Email format verification tools

Before sending to any contact, verify the email exists:
- **Hunter.io** (free tier: 25 searches/month) — paste domain, find email patterns
- **Voila Norbert** (free trial: 50 searches) — similar
- **Clearbit Connect** (free Gmail extension) — shows verified emails in Gmail

Or just try the email pattern and see if it bounces. Bounce rate is a tolerable cost.

## What to do if they don't respond

After 5 business days, one follow-up. If no response, move on. Do NOT:
- Send 3+ follow-ups
- CC their boss
- Call them at home
- Reach out on personal social media (Instagram, Facebook)

That's the line between persistence and harassment. Stay on the right side.
