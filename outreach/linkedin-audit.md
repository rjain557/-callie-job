Your LinkedIn is the most valuable asset in a job search — 90% of recruiters use it
as their first filter. Spend 2 hours optimizing it and every future outreach becomes
easier.

Target profile: **www.linkedin.com/in/callie-wells17**

## HEADLINE (most important — shows in every search)
