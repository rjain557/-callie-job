# Template 3 — Alumni / Former Colleague Reconnect

## When to use
For former colleagues at Ethan Allen, Goff Designs, or Vintage Design Inc. who have
since left for other companies. LinkedIn is perfect for identifying these people — the
"Where [Company] employees went next" feature.

## Subject line options
- `Ex-Ethan Allen — quick hello`
- `Fellow [Company Alum] — happy to reconnect`
- `Small world — are you still at [Current Company]?`

## Email body

```
Hi [First Name],

Long time! We worked together at [Company Name — Ethan Allen / Goff / Vintage] back
in [year — "2023-2024" / "when I was on the Design team"]. I noticed you've been at
[Their Current Company] for [X years / months] and wanted to reach out.

Quick update on me: I'm exploring my next move after three years at Ethan Allen
($800K+ in design sales, Gold Spirit Award in 2025), and I'm thinking carefully about
where to land next — ideally a design-forward showroom, residential firm, or
consultative role.

A few questions if you have a minute:
1. How's [Current Company] been? Any feel for whether they hire design consultants?
2. If not — any firms you've come across lately that I should look at?
3. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute catch-up call?

No pressure at all. Always happy to return the favor if I can.

Cheers,
Callie
949-557-9014
linkedin.com/in/callie-wells17
```

## Rules
- **Lead with the shared history** — it's your warmth hook
- **Brief update on you** — context without bragging
- **2-3 specific questions** — easier to respond than open-ended "any leads?"
- **Low-pressure close** — don't make them feel obligated
- **Offer value back** — sets the right tone

## How to find alumni on LinkedIn
1. Go to the company's LinkedIn page (e.g., Ethan Allen)
2. Click "People" tab
3. Filter by "Past companies"
4. Sort by "2nd connections" or mutual connections first
5. Open DMs to 5-10 warmest per week

## What to do next
If they respond positively:
- Offer 15 min, pick 2 time slots
- Come prepared with 2-3 specific questions
- Ask if they'd be willing to make a direct intro (only if relevant)
- Send a thank-you note within 24 hrs
