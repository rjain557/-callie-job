# Company Intel — Waterworks

## Snapshot
- **HQ:** Danbury, CT — but strong West Coast presence
- **Ownership:** Acquired by Morgan Stanley in 2016
- **Showrooms:** Newport Beach, Los Angeles, San Francisco on West Coast
- **What they sell:** Premium bathroom and kitchen fixtures, fittings, tile, lighting
- **Price point:** Entry $2K / full bath $30-50K+ / high-end projects $100K+

## Why this is a great Callie target
1. **RH Channel program** — Restoration Hardware partnership means RH clients get
   referred to Waterworks. That's exactly Callie's past clientele.
2. **Trade-only focus** — Designers and architects are primary customers, not
   walk-ins. This means the consultative, relationship-driven sales Callie excels at.
3. **Luxury positioning** — $100/faucet is entry. Clients expect experts, not order
   takers.
4. **High commission ceiling** — successful consultants can make $120K+ all-in

## Key positions to target
- **Design Consultant, RH Channel** (Newport Beach — Callie already applied 4/14)
- **Trade Sales Consultant**
- **Showroom Specialist**
- **Trade Account Manager**

## Current climate (as of 2026)
- Morgan Stanley has been patient with growth; brand integrity emphasized
- West Coast market seen as growth frontier
- They value people who stay long-term (3-5 yrs typical tenure for top performers)
- Hiring slowed somewhat in 2026 but always looking for proven design-sales talent

## Interview culture
- **Expect:** Phone screen with recruiter → Skills/Design interview → Final with
  Showroom Director or Regional
- **They value:** Technical knowledge (can learn), aesthetic sensibility (essential),
  relationship skills (key differentiator)
- **Dress code:** Polished, showroom-professional (match their clients)
- **Culture fit signals:** Can you talk about tile, marble, brass finishes with
  specifics? Do you understand the trade's pace (weeks to close a designer, not
  minutes)?

## Compensation expectations
- Design Consultant RH Channel: $55-70K base + commission

- Year 2-3 top performer: $120-150K
- Benefits: Standard MS-backed corporate benefits package

## Negotiation intel
- Waterworks pays slightly above Ethan Allen base
- Commission structure is structured and fair
- They match market on health/401k
- 2-3 weeks PTO starting, scales with tenure

## How to apply
**Ideal:** Direct email to Showroom Director at Newport Beach location
**Good:** Apply via waterworks.com/careers, then follow up with direct email
**Backup:** Through a design industry recruiter (JBK Associates)

## Red flags
- If they push for "retail sales" experience over "design consulting" — wrong role
  for Callie

- If they're filling role after <6 months — high turnover signal
