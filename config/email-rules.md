# Email Rules - MANDATORY for ALL emails sent to Callie

## Format for Job Kit Emails

Every email to Callie about a job MUST follow this exact format:

```
Subject: APPLY HERE: {Company} - {Title} ({Pay or "See Listing"})

Body:

Hi Callie,

APPLY HERE:
{DIRECT URL TO THE JOB POSTING - NOT a search URL}

Company: {Company Name}
Role: {Job Title}
Location: {City, CA} ({distance}mi from RSM)
Pay: {Salary/hourly or "Not listed"}
Score: {X.X}/5

Steps:
1. Click the link above
2. Hit Apply on {LinkedIn/Indeed/Company Site}
3. Upload the attached PDF when it asks for your resume

The attached PDF has your cover letter (page 1) and {resume type} resume (page 2).

{1-2 sentences about why this is a good fit for her}

- Your Job Search Pipeline
```

## CRITICAL RULES

1. **ALWAYS include the direct job URL** — never a search URL, never omit it
2. **URL must be on its own line** right after "APPLY HERE:" so it's clickable
3. **ALWAYS attach the merged PDF** (cover letter + resume combined)
4. **Send ONE email per job** — never batch multiple jobs in one email
5. **Include the apply steps** — tell her which platform (LinkedIn/Indeed/etc)
6. **If the posting expired**, say so and provide the company website or a search link as fallback
7. **Never send a blank email** — if the body fails, do not send

## Direct URL Sources

When finding jobs, ALWAYS capture the direct URL:
- LinkedIn: `linkedin.com/jobs/view/{slug}-{id}`
- Indeed: `indeed.com/viewjob?jk={job_key}`
- ZipRecruiter: full URL from the posting
- Company sites: the direct careers/job page URL

If you only have a search URL, go fetch the actual posting URL before sending.
